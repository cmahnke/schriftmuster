Die Weihnachtsgeschichte

Since the Advent season begin: The Christmas story in woodcuts.

In case a reader is wondering why the Christmas story is presented on this blog: The author of the book is the typographer Rudolf Koch , starting in spring 2021 some of his (Fraktur) fonts will be presented here. He is also the creator of the font Neuland , which is used for the headings in the sister blog Backsteinexpressionismus comes.
