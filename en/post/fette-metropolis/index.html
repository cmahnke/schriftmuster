D. Stempel: Fette Metropolis

The back of the ‘Die deutsche Zeitung ihr Werden, Wesen und Wirken’ offers a type specimen from the type foundry D. Stempel.

The name of the font is ‘Fette Metropolis’, like the well-known film. Incidentally, a new Superman film opens in cinemas today.

The rest of the publication can be found at Brick Expressionism.
